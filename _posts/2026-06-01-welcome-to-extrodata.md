## Why EXD Exists

The data and AI consulting market is crowded. Most firms operate the same model: sell strategy, hand off to implementation partners, and leave your team to figure out the last mile.

We built Extrodata because we believe a different approach is possible — one where the people who design the solution are the same people who build it, test it, and hand it over to your team.

## What Makes Us Different

**Senior-only delivery.** Every EXD practitioner has 10+ years of hands-on experience. There are no juniors on our projects, no learning curves charged to your budget, and no handoffs between strategy and implementation.

**End-to-end capability.** From data strategy through architecture to AI model deployment — we own the full stack. This means fewer vendors, clearer accountability, and faster time to value.

**Outcomes over activity.** We measure our success by the impact we create: reporting cycles shortened, costs reduced, revenue unlocked. Not by the number of workshops we run or diagrams we produce.

## What We're Seeing in 2026

The organisations getting real value from AI share three characteristics:

1. **They started with data foundations.** Generative AI and agentic workflows amplify whatever data you have — good or bad. The winners invested in clean, accessible, governed data first.

2. **They prioritised explainability.** In regulated industries especially, "the AI decided" is not an acceptable answer. The best implementations include transparent reasoning and human oversight at critical decision points.

3. **They built internal capability.** The most successful engagements leave the client team stronger than they found them. Knowledge transfer isn't a line item — it's the entire point.
