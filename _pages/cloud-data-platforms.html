Cloud

Your cloud data platform is the engine room of your analytics.

Choosing the right cloud platform — and configuring it correctly — determines whether your data organisation thrives or struggles with cost, performance, and complexity. We design platforms that are right-sized for your workload, governed for security, and optimised for total cost of ownership.

Whether you're starting fresh on a single cloud or consolidating a multi-cloud sprawl, we bring deep platform expertise across AWS, Azure, and GCP — so you can focus on insights, not infrastructure.

What We Deliver

Cloud Data Warehouse Design

We architect cloud warehouses that deliver blazing query performance at optimal cost. From schema design to resource allocation, every decision is tuned for your specific workload patterns.

  • Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, and Synapse architecture
  • Warehouse sizing, clustering, and partitioning strategy
  • Query performance tuning and cost optimisation
  • Cross-database replication and federation patterns

Lakehouse Architecture

The lakehouse pattern unifies the best of data lakes and warehouses — giving you the flexibility to store any data format with the performance and ACID guarantees your analysts need.

  • Delta Lake, Apache Iceberg, and Hudi implementations
  • Unified batch and streaming architectures
  • Data mesh patterns for decentralised ownership
  • Open table format migration from proprietary systems

Multi-Cloud Strategy

Vendor lock-in is a real risk. We design multi-cloud architectures that give you the freedom to choose the best tool for each job — while maintaining data portability and consistent governance.

  • Multi-cloud data platform architecture design
  • Cross-cloud data replication and synchronisation
  • Cost comparison and workload placement optimisation
  • Disaster recovery and business continuity planning

Platform Operations & Support

A platform is only as good as its operations. We provide ongoing management, monitoring, and optimisation — so your data platform stays performant, secure, and cost-effective.

  • 24/7 platform monitoring and incident response
  • Cost tracking, alerting, and right-sizing recommendations
  • Security patching, access reviews, and compliance audits
  • Capacity planning and performance benchmarking
