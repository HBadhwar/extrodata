Financial Services

Tier 1 Bank — Regulatory Reporting Automation

A leading UK bank needed to modernise its regulatory reporting pipeline, which relied on manual Excel processes across 12 business units.

42% Reduction in reporting time
£2.3M Annual cost savings
600+ Hours saved per quarter
12 Business units consolidated

The Challenge

Regulatory returns were compiled manually from disparate source systems, creating operational risk and leaving no capacity for analysis. The bank needed a reliable, auditable pipeline that could scale with increasing regulatory demands.

Our Approach

We designed and deployed an automated data pipeline using dbt and Snowflake, with built-in quality checks and lineage tracking. EXD embedded senior engineers alongside the bank's existing team for knowledge transfer.

The Outcome

Reporting cycles shortened from weeks to days. The team now uses freed capacity for predictive analysis rather than data wrangling — and the solution has been extended to cover additional regulatory frameworks.
