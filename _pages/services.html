Engagement Models

Project-Based

Fixed-scope, fixed-timeline engagements with clear deliverables. Ideal for well-defined initiatives like a data platform migration or a specific AI model deployment.

Best for: Defined projects with known outcomes and budget parameters.

Retainer / Advisory

Ongoing strategic partnership with dedicated EXD principals on retainer. Continuous guidance, prioritisation, and hands-on delivery across multiple workstreams.

Best for: Organisations building long-term data capability with sustained expert support.

Embedded Team

EXD senior practitioners integrate directly into your teams, adopting your ways of working while bringing specialist expertise you may not have in-house.

Best for: Scaling delivery capacity with senior talent that augments your existing team.
