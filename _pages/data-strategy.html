Foundational Service

Your data strategy is the foundation of everything that follows.

Most organisations sit on terabytes of untapped data. Without a coherent strategy, that data becomes a liability — siloed, inconsistent, and impossible to trust. We work with your leadership team to define a clear vision for how data will drive decisions, reduce risk, and create competitive advantage.

Our architecture designs are pragmatic and evolutionary — we build roadmaps that deliver quick wins in quarter one while laying the groundwork for transformative capability over 12 to 24 months.

What We Deliver

Enterprise Data Strategy

We align your data ambitions to business objectives, regulatory requirements, and technical reality. The output is a living strategy document — not a shelf piece — that your team can execute against with confidence.

  • Current-state assessment across people, process, and technology
  • Target-state vision with phased delivery roadmap
  • Business case development with quantified ROI per phase
  • Stakeholder alignment workshops and change management planning

Target-State Architecture

We design architectures that are fit for purpose today and adaptable for tomorrow. Whether you need a data lake, warehouse, lakehouse, or mesh pattern — we recommend the right fit and show you how to get there.

  • Data platform architecture: lakes, warehouses, lakehouses
  • Integration patterns for hybrid and multi-cloud environments
  • Real-time and batch processing architecture design
  • Reference architectures tailored to your industry and scale
  • PCI-DSS compliant data architecture for regulated industries

Data Governance Frameworks

Governance is the discipline that turns data from a cost centre into a trusted asset. We establish ownership models, quality standards, and compliance processes that your organisation can actually live with.

  • Data ownership and stewardship models
  • Quality standards, monitoring, and remediation workflows
  • GDPR, CCPA, and industry-specific compliance mapping
  • Master data management (MDM) strategy and implementation

Roadmap & Prioritisation

We translate strategy into action with a prioritised delivery roadmap. Every initiative is scoped, costed, and tied to a measurable business outcome so your leadership team can make informed investment decisions.

  • Initiative backlog with effort, cost, and impact scoring
  • Quick-win identification for early momentum and buy-in
  • Resource planning and capability gap analysis
  • Vendor selection support and procurement guidance
