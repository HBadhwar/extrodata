Insight Service

Data without insight is just storage.

The best data platform in the world is worthless if your people can't extract meaning from it. We design analytics experiences that meet your stakeholders where they are — from boardroom dashboards for the C-suite to self-service tools for operational teams.

We go beyond building reports. We establish KPI frameworks, train your teams in data literacy, and create a culture where decisions are driven by evidence, not intuition.

What We Deliver

Executive Dashboards

We design dashboards that tell a story at a glance. Every visual is purposeful, every metric is trusted, and every insight is actionable — so your leadership team can make decisions with confidence.

  • C-suite executive scorecards and KPI dashboards
  • Department-level operational dashboards
  • Real-time monitoring and alerting views
  • Mobile-optimised designs for on-the-go access

Self-Service Analytics

We empower your business users to answer their own questions — without becoming dependent on the data team. Curated datasets, intuitive tools, and clear governance mean faster insights at scale.

  • Curated semantic layers for non-technical users
  • Self-service tool selection and deployment (Power BI, Tableau, Looker)
  • Data catalogue implementation for discoverability
  • Governed access controls and row-level security

KPI Frameworks & Metric Stores

When different departments define "revenue" or "churn" differently, decision-making breaks down. We establish a single source of truth for your key metrics so everyone is working from the same numbers.

  • Enterprise KPI dictionary with standardised definitions
  • Metric store implementation (e.g., dbt metrics, MetricFlow)
  • Cascading OKR frameworks from board to team level
  • Automated metric validation and drift detection

Data Literacy Programmes

Tools alone don't create a data-driven culture. We run training programmes that build confidence and competence across your organisation — from board members to front-line analysts.

  • Role-based training: executives, managers, analysts
  • Hands-on workshops with your own data and tools
  • Data storytelling and visualisation best practices
  • Ongoing coaching and community-of-practice setup
