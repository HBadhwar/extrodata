Financial Services

From regulatory reporting automation to real-time fraud detection and credit risk modelling. We help banks, insurers, and asset managers turn data into competitive advantage while meeting the strictest compliance standards. Our work spans Basel III/IV reporting, AML transaction monitoring, PCI-DSS compliant payment data architectures, and AI-driven customer personalisation.

Healthcare & Life Sciences

Clinical data integration, real-world evidence generation, and predictive analytics for patient outcomes. We navigate HIPAA, GDPR, and industry-specific regulations while building solutions that improve care delivery.

Media & Advertising

We have worked with global media holding companies and leading independent agencies to build the data infrastructure that underpins cross-channel campaign measurement, audience segmentation, and marketing performance reporting. From ingesting paid media APIs to implementing account-per-domain data mesh architectures, we understand the structural complexity of agency data environments.

Gaming & Betting

Gaming and betting operators manage some of the highest-velocity, most regulation-intensive data environments in any industry. We bring hands-on experience from tier-one global operators — covering real-time event processing, responsible gambling analytics, regulatory reporting, and customer behaviour modelling at scale.

Retail & eCommerce

Demand forecasting, personalised recommendations, and supply chain optimisation. We help retailers unify online and offline data to create seamless customer experiences and maximise lifetime value.

Manufacturing

Predictive maintenance, quality control through computer vision, and production optimisation. We connect OT and IT data to give manufacturers end-to-end visibility across their operations.

Public Sector

Modernising citizen services through data-driven decision making. From benefits fraud detection to resource allocation optimisation, we help government organisations deliver better outcomes with transparent, accountable data practices that meet public sector governance requirements.
