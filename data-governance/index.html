Data Governance

Ensure data quality, security, and regulatory compliance.

Data governance is the foundation of trusted data. We help organizations establish policies, processes, and technologies that ensure their data is accurate, secure, and compliant with regulatory requirements.

What We Offer

  • Data governance framework design
  • Data quality management and monitoring
  • Master data management (MDM)
  • Data privacy and compliance (GDPR, CCPA)
  • Metadata management and data cataloging

Technologies

We implement governance solutions using Collibra, Alation, Azure Purview, Snowflake Data Sharing, and custom-built tools tailored to your organization's needs.
