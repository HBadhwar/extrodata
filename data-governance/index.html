Data Governance

Frameworks that make your data trustworthy and audit-ready.

Data governance is the discipline that separates reliable data from unreliable data. We help organizations establish the policies, processes, and tooling that ensure their data is accurate, secure, and compliant — so every decision is backed by information you can trust.

What We Build

  • Governance framework design — roles, responsibilities, and decision rights mapped to your organization
  • Data quality standards with automated monitoring and breach alerting
  • Master data management (MDM) for consistent customer, product, and entity records
  • Privacy and compliance programs aligned to GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, or SOX requirements
  • Metadata management and data cataloging with Collibra, Alation, or Azure Purview

Our Approach

We do not impose generic frameworks. We assess your current data landscape, identify the risks and gaps, then design a governance program that fits your regulatory environment and operational reality. Every policy we write is paired with the tooling that enforces it.

Technologies

Collibra, Alation, Azure Purview, Snowflake Data Sharing & RBAC, Great Expectations, custom Python validation frameworks.
