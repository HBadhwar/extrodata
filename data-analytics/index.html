Data Analytics

Analytics platforms built for clarity and precision.

We design analytics platforms that give organizations operational clarity. Our approach starts with understanding your business questions, then we build the models, dashboards, and self-serve tools that answer them reliably.

What We Build

  • Executive dashboards and operational reporting built on Snowflake or Databricks
  • Predictive models for demand forecasting, churn analysis, and revenue optimization
  • Self-serve analytics environments that empower business teams without engineering dependency
  • Data visualization with Tableau, Power BI, or Looker — chosen to fit your stack
  • Statistical modeling and A/B testing frameworks for product and marketing decisions

Our Approach

We do not ship dashboards and walk away. We define the metrics that matter, build the models that calculate them correctly, and train your team to maintain and extend them. Every analytics deliverable includes documentation, data lineage, and a handover plan.

Technologies

Snowflake, Databricks, dbt, Tableau, Power BI, Looker, Python (Pandas, scikit-learn), R, Azure Synapse, Apache Spark.
